About the project -

The project aims to give insights on a Student's Performance by performing Exploratory Data Analysis on the dataset provided for it.
The dataset contains the marks secured by students in various subjects like reading, writing and math. The objective of the analysis was to understand how the student's test scores are affected by the other variables like Gender, Ethnicity, Parental level of education and other background data provided. 
- It is checked that the data is clean.
- Univariate Analysis is performed on the data.
- Followed by the Univariate, Bivariate Analysis is performed.
- All the inferences and conclusions observed from the visualizations are listed clearly in simple words.
